How To Write A Software Developer Resume?

Here is a list of steps you can follow when writing your own software developer resume:

1. Start with your contact information

The first section to add to your software developer resume is one that presents your contact information. This can provide hiring managers with ways to contact you, in case they want to set up an interview. You can include details like your name, your phone number, your email address and your city and state in this section. Be sure to use current information, like your most recent location and a professional email address that you check fairly often, so you receive any important communications from potential jobs.

2. Craft a professional summary

After your contact information, write a professional summary. This is a brief paragraph that discusses your most relevant qualifications and experiences. For example, a software developer may write about the types of programming languages they know, the number of years they have worked in software development or the types of professional certifications they have. It can be helpful to keep your professional summary between one and three sentences, as this can allow hiring managers to read it quickly and learn about you right when they open your resume.Â
